12" x 6" Custom License Plate

Our custom license plates have pre-drilled holes and are made of quality light weight aluminum. We make each license plate via a process called dye-sublimation which produces sharp vivid colors. We can imprint almost any artwork and the results are always amazing.

Customization

We can customize our license plates with your logo or your artwork. We use a dyb-sublimation process to imprint the signs. In a nutshell, dye-sublimation is the process of imprinting ink onto a surface using heat. This method gives our license plates a glossy professonal photo-like look.
