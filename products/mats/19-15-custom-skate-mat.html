19" x 15" Custom Skate Mat

If you want a soft and dry surface to rest your feet post game then you'll love your skate mats.

We can customize your skate mats with your logo, message, image, or any combination of the three. Additionally we can personalize each skate mat with a name, a number, etc..

Our skate mats are 1/4" thick, look great, and are made of rubber mousepad material.

Customization

We make two kinds of skate mats - personalized and custom. If your order is for skate mats that have the same design, then we classify the skates mats in your order as custom. If each mat in your order uses a different design, then we classify the skate mats in your order as personalized. Personalized skate mats take longer to make and so cost a bit more.
