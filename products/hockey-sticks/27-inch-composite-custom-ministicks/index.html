27" Hockey Ministicks (Composite)

Our composite hockey ministicks are ideal for knee hockey. Customizable with your logo/artwork.

See the following options for more details:

  1. Vinyl Labeled
  2. Blank

Vinyl Labeled

We customize our player and goalie composite sticks using a high quality vinyl label which has a scuff resistant clear laminate on top to give our sticks their professional shiny look. On the player sticks the vinyl label wraps around the shaft. On the goalie stick the vinyl label is applied on one side of the stick. An optional second vinyl label can be applied to the opposite side.
