8" Table-Top Hockey Ministicks (Plastic)

They are small, awesome and made in Canada! You can add them to your key chain. You can use them as a promotional item. Our table top ministicks are a smaller version of our popular 17" plastic ministicks.

See the following options for more details:

  1. Digitally Printed
  2. Blank

Digitally Printed

Digital printing is one of the methods we use to customize your table-top ministicks with your artwork or logo. Our digital printer prints your artwork directly onto the surface of the table-top ministicks in full color and cures the ink using UV light for a lasting imprint. We can print on the full surface of the table-top ministick.

Note: Only one side of the table-top ministicks can be digitally printed on
