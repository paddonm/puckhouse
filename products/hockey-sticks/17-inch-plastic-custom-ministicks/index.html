17" Hockey Ministicks (Plastic)

Our 17-inch plastic custom ministicks are our most popular ministicks. We've made thousands of them for hockey leagues across Canada and US. Hockey leagues iften used them as gifts at tournaments and fundraisers. Companies use them to promote events, products and brands. You may even find our custom ministicks at birthdays, bar mitzvahs and weddings.

See the following options for more details:

  1. Digitally Printed
  2. Screen Printed
  3. Blank

Digitally Printed

Digital printing is one of the methods we use to customize our 17" hockey ministicks with your artwork or logo. Our digital printer prints your custom artwork/design directly onto the surface of the ministicks in full color and cures the ink using UV light for a lasting imprint. We can print on the full surface of the ministick.

Note: Only one side of the ministicks (player and goalie) can be digitally printed on
