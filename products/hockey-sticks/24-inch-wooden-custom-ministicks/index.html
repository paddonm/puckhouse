24" Hockey Ministicks (Wood)

Sometimes going with a classic wooden ministick is better. Our 24 inch wooden ministicks are customizable and are often used as gifts/souvenirs at hockey tournaments. Some customers get them for hockey themed weddings.

See the following options for more details:

  1. Digitally Printed
  2. Laser Etched
  3. Blank

Digitally Printed

We can customize our wooden ministicks with your logo/artwork. We imprint your logo/artwork in full color with our digital printer. Only one side of the ministick can currently be printed on.

Please note that some designs look fine printed directly to the wooden sticks. However, others look better if white is printed first on the stick and then your design. We have the capability to print white and offer that option to you.
