Custom Hockey Pucks

Welcome to OGP's Puck Printing section. We both pad print (Spot Colors) and digital print (CMYK) pucks which allows us to select the best possible method to meet your needs. Our pricing and quality is unmatched. We are the exclusive puck supplier to the OHL, WHL, QMJHL, USHL and many other leagues across North America. We can handle any order larger or small. Printing on pucks isn't easy.

Trust a company with the expertise and experience acquired by printing millions of pucks.

Pad Printed

With pad printing we can print up to 6 individual colors on each side of the puck. A printing plate is made for each color. Inks used in this process are highly pigmented so a very thin layer of ink is achieved.

Digitally Printed

With our large format printer, we can digitally print your pucks in full color. White is printed first then the colors are printed on top in your standard CMYK process. This process is ideal for short runs or printing images or orders with more than 6 colors.
