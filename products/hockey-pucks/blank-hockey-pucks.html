Viceroy

In response to rising domestic puck pricing, we've added what we call the Viceroy SL. Technically, it's an imported puck manufactured in Sri Lanka but it is formulated here in Canada by Viceroy. Viceroy has been a puck manufacturer in Canada for decades and locating the manufacturing in Sri Lanka is no accident. That part of the world has access to natural rubber sources that haven't been cost effective to tap into domestically for a long time. The end result is a quality puck that still has CANADA proudly molded on the side. Based on our testing, this puck is suitable for all applications. It's best used frozen to minimize chipping.

The blank Viceroy puck can be customized! See our custom printed hockey pucks for details.

Nonprints

During the manufacture of our Canadian made pucks, they are individually inspected and any puck that has a slight chip, small pinholes or some other minor surface defect is set aside. They are often referred to as non-prints or factory seconds. They are still offiical weight and size and are suitable for game play. We most commonly sell these as practice pucks to various levels of hockey including the Canadian Hockey League.
