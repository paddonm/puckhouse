3'x5' Flag

Our large 5' x 3' flags come complete with a header and two brass grommets to fit typical installations. They are made of 100D polyester (75 gsm) and weigh 110 grams each. They are printed in full color making the design possibilities endless. They make an excellent retail item. A great item to hang up in the man cave. Due to our production process, typical lead times are 4-6 weeks. However, if you require them faster than that, please contact us.

Digitally Printed

For short runs we digitally print the flags. They are printed in full color and have about an 80% bleed through to the opposite side.

Screen Printed

For larger orders, it becomes economical to offer screen printed flags. Bleed through is nearly 100%. An ideal choice for a traditional national flag. Pricing is based on 2 colors (plus white).
