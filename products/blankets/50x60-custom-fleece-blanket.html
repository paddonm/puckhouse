Fleece Blanket (Custom)

Our soft fleece blankets are ideal for keeping you warm either at home or at that rink that never seems to have their heaters on. And folded up, it makes a nice seat cushion for those cold hard metal and wooden benches. The blanket measures 50" x 60" in size and is made right here in Canada. Your team logo is embroidered directly on the blanket and it comes with a hand strap to make it easy to carry around. It makes a great retail item or a gift that any hockey mom would love to have.

Currently available in black, blue, dark blue, red and grey.
