Hip Flask

It's made of stainless steel, it's curved, stylish and awesome! The hip flask makes a great wedding, graduation, birthday or anniversary gift. It can also be used to promote your tournament, fundraiser, event, or business. We can laser engrave or imprint your logo/artwork on the hip flask.

See the following sections for more details:

  1. Engraved
  2. Imprinted
  • Color: Black (Matte-Like Finish)
  • Material: Stainless Steel
  • Size: 8 oz
  • Dimentions: 3.5" x 5.25" x 0.75" (L x H x W)

Engraved

We customize the engraved hip flask by laser etching your logo/artwork directly on the surface of the flask itself. The etching/engaving looks gold-like. Currently we engrave on a single side of the hip flask.

Note: Only one side of the hip flask is engraved.
