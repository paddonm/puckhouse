Licence Plate Covers (Blank)

If you just need blank licence plate covers, we have you covered too. We have three different types of licence plate covers.

Type 10153 fits Ontario and Nova Scotia licence plates.

Type 10155 fits Alberta, Saskatchewan, Ontario, Nova Scotia, PIE and Newfoundland licence plates.

Type 10156 fits all licence plates.
