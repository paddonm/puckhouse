Bull Bell

Make noise, be loud at your next event!

Use the Bull Bell to make some noise! It is ideal for sporting events but also parties, rallys and weddings. Use the Bull Bell to create an electrifying atmosphere at your sporting event. Use it to cheer and support your team.

The Bull Bell comes in variety of colors and we can customize it with your logo. See details below.

Hear the Bull Bell!

Details

Bull Bell

  • Plastic handle
  • Metal body
  • Metal ball clapper
  • Makes metalic bell-like sound
  • Approximate size: 4" x 2½" x 9½" (L x W x H)
  • Color options: white, black, blue, red & yellow

The Bull Bell can be customized with your logo. Send us your vector logo in ESP, PDF, AI, or CDR format. We will print it on a vinyl sheet and die cut the vinyl to the shape of your logo and add it on the face of the Bull Bell.

If you have questions about customization, please contact us.
