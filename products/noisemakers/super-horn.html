Super Horn

Make super noise with the Super Horn. It's loud! It looks awesome and is easy to use.

The Super Horn is an air horn similar to the Vuvuzela. Great for stadiums and arenas. Perfect for making noise at sporting events but also Mardi Gras, carnivals, homecomings and parties.

The Super Horn comes in variety of colors and we can customize it with your logo. See details below.

Hear the Super Horn!

Details

  • Made of plastic
  • Consists of two parts: horn and mouthpiece.
  • Removable mouthpiece.
  • Works and sounds like an air horn
  • Comes with 16" nylon lanyard
  • Approximate size: 21" (L)
  • Color options: black, blue, red & yellow

The Super Horn can be customized with your logo. See the "Cutomization" tab for details.

To get a quote or place an order please see the "Order Process" tab.

The Super Horn can be customized with your logo. Send us your vector logo in ESP, PDF, AI, or CDR format. We will print it on a vinyl sheet and die cut the vinyl to the shape of your logo and add just above the bell (wider part) on the Super Horn. See product images above.

If you have questions about customization, please contact us.
