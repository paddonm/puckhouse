11" Hand Clapper

The Hand Clapper is a plastic hand-shaped sports noisemaker 11" in length. It's perfect for promoting, cheering and supporting sports teams. Often used at school sport games but also parades and parties.

Comes in several different colors. Features three hands on each clapper and makes clapping sound by shaking it. Very light and easy to use.

We can customize the Hand Clapper with your logo. See details below.

Hear the Hand Clapper!

Details

Hand Clapper

  • Made of soft colorful plastic
  • Each clapper has 3 hands
  • Very light
  • Makes clapping sound
  • Approximate size: 5" x 1" x 11" (L x W x H)
  • Color options: black, blue, red & yellow

The Hand Clapper can be customized with your logo. Send us your vector logo in ESP, PDF, AI, or CDR format. We will print it on a vinyl sheet and die cut the vinyl to the shape of your logo and add it on the hand of the Hand Clapper. See product images above.

If you have questions about customization, please contact us.
