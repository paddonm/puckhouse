Cheering Sticks

Cheering sticks are perfect noisemakers for sporting events, parties, parades, rallies, promotion and other special events. They are reusable, easy to carry, and easy to use. Inflate and clap two sticks together to make some noise!

To promote your business, association or even we customize your cheering sticks with your logo or message/text.

For more information, see the details, customization, price sections below.

Details

Cheering Sticks

  • Size: 23.75" x 4.75" (H x W)
  • Come in pair
  • Come with a straw to inflate
  • Reusable
  • Base colors: white, blue, red, yellow, black, green, orange, maroon and pink.

We customize the cheering sticks by printing your logo and text directly on the surface of the cheering sticks. We imprint one color at at time.

To customize your cheering sticks, first chose the color of your cheering sticks, then the imprint color(s). The cheering sticks come in pairs, however, you may choose a different color for each cheering stick in the pair. Send us your logo, text and color choice and will do the rest.

If you have any questions about customization, please contact us.
