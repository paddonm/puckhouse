15x24 Spearhead Pennant (Custom)

The spearhead pennant is a great way to promote your team or celebrate a championship. This pennant is 15 by 24 inches, it is made of felt material and can be customized with your team logo, colors and message. Only one side of the pennant is imprinted. We imprint in full color.
