Custom Skate Towel

Our white skate towels are imprinted in vibrant full color. They make a great custom skate towel, golf towel or a rally towel for fans. A very affordable giveaway for tournaments.

Details

The towels are made of 100% polyester and measure 10" x 20" (25 cm x 50 cm). The images are printed using dye sublimation and will not fade, peel off or crack after use or washing. The image is dyed into the fabric, not printed on top of the fabric as done with screen printing.
