ALL WHITE OFFICIALLY LICENSED PRODUCT REVERSE

(2001 - 2002)

 

The reverse logo was updated once again reverting back to an all white design. It is different from the original all-white from a few years earlier. It is also the last year where the 'AHL' located at the bottom of each front logo was printed in lower case. It was updated the following year to capital letters.
