IN GLAS CO ADVERTISEMENT REVERSE

(1991 - 1998)

 

The In Glas Co became the official game puck slug in 1991 to mirror the commitment made by the NHL. The game pucks typically had local advertising on the back as shown below. A new game ring design replaced this series in 1998, however advertisements continued to be issued on the back.
