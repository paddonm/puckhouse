EDMONTON OILERS

(1973 - Present)

 

The charter franchise in the WHA from Edmonton went by the name of the Alberta Oilers. In year two, they updated it to the Edmonton Oilers. The team outlasted the WHA joining the NHL in 1979. They brought with them a young star named Wayne Gretzky who had been purchased from the Indianapolis Racers. Gretzky was to rewrite the NHL record books and lead Edmonton to several Stanley Cups.
