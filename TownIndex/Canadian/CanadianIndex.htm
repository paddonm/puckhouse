CANADIAN TOWN INDEX

While listing pucks by league works well for professional hockey, it doesn't work quite as well for amateur hockey in Canada. The number of leagues and teams are numerous and they change frequently. Also, it is very likely that a person will have an amateur puck but not have any idea what league it is from. As a result, a town index has been created.

To locate a team, select the appropriate province or territory in which the town is located:
