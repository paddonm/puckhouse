TORONTO MAPLE LEAFS

(1927 - Present)

In 1927, the Toronto St. Pats of the NHL were renamed the Maple Leafs. The Leafs have went on to win many Stanley Cups although their last one was back in 1967. The team is still member of the NHL today.
