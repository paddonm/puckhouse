CINCINNATI CYCLONES

(1990 - 2001)

 

The Cincinnati Cyclones started out as an expansion team in the ECHL in 1990. After two seasons, the team made the unusual move of jumping leagues joining the IHL. The team folded on June 5, 2001 and the league followed suit shortly after. A new Cyclones were created in the ECHL the following season but they are not a continuation of this team.
