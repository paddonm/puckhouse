PULSAR PUCKS

(1996 - 1998)

Pulsar pucks, not to be confused with the Fox-Trax puck used in games, is a souvenir puck. They are manufactured in Sri Lanka and have patent information molded into on of the surfaces. They have two reflectors on the side of the puck. Adding to the confusion is Fox's use of the puck as a souvenir as a take-off on their game puck model.
