IN GLAS CO - AUTOGRAPH SERIES

(1997 - 1998)

In Glas Co issued a series of souvenir pucks that a team crest on the front and a place for signatures on the back. Each back was team specific so to see the reverse of a given puck, hold the cursor over the targeted puck until it appears. Some teams were issued prior to starting play.
