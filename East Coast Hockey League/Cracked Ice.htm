Cracked Ice - Puck World

(1999 - 2002)

Puck World issued a series pucks often referred to as the 'Cracked Ice' series. This style of souvenir puck was issued for various teams in various leagues. A few were issued with local advertising on the back (Pee Dee, Hampton Roads, Charlotte) and they can also be found issued with blank backs. All years have been grouped since not all teams were issued.
