C & T Hockey Products Official Game Puck

2002 - 2003

Various ECHL teams expressed interest in continuing to acquire game pucks from C&T. The league stipulated that the pucks had to look virtually identical to what was being supplied by In Glas Co to allow that to happen. The result was another attractive white ring design with a silver ring reverse. Two different signatures can be found on the back. A few were also issued with ads.
