Red Shield Reverse

(1996 - 1997)

 

The league continued to issue pucks with the updated league logo on the back for the 1996-97 season. One noticeable change was that the back logo was now screened in red. One team (Roanoke) featured a slight variation of the back logo by having the added notation 'C&T Advertising Products' below the logo. The slugs used appeared to narrow somewhat to either General Tire of Vegum/Slovakia.
