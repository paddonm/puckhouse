OFFICIAL OHA VICEROY REVERSE

A - Collingwood

(1970 - 1987)

The most common back found in Ontario is the white screened Official Viceroy back. This is a popular back logo for collectors and warrants its own section. The number of pucks issued over the years with this back logo is huge. A few pucks shown in this section are not found elsewhere on the site because the information on the team shown is not currently known.
