MEMORIAL CUP

(1919 - Present)

The Memorial Cup was donated by the Ontario Hockey Association to serve as a trophy to honor the best junior hockey team in Canada and to honor the young men who served in WWI. It gained immediate acceptance and the Memorial Cup took its place along side the Allan Cup and Stanley Cup as the most coveted trophies in hockey.
