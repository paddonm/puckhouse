OHA REVERSE

(1975 - 1981)

Pucks were also issued with the OHA logo on the back. The league parted ways with the OHA in 1981. Front logos were generally the same as those issued with CMJHL logos on the back. Viceroy slugs were used. Some of the front logos can also be found issued with blank backs.
