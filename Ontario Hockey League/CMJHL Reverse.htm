SMALL CMJHL REVERSE

(1972 - 1981)

The OHA became one of three leagues that made up the new Canadian Major Junior Hockey League in 1972. Pucks were issued with the small CMJHL logo on the back (The OHA did not have any of the large CMJHL back logos unlike the WCHL and QMJHL). Various Viceroy slugs were used. Souvenir pucks were typically screened in full color while souvenir pucks were most commonly issued in two-color screening.
