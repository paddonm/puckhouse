In Glas Co Bettman 2000 Series

(1999 - 2000)

 

The NHL commemorated the new millenium by putting a special logo on the backs of their game pucks for the 1999-2000 season. In Glas Co's first production run featured a glaring error. They omitted the company name on the reverse logo. This was corrected on later production runs.
