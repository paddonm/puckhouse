In Glas Co Bettman Series

(1992 - 1995)

 

Gary Bettman replaced Gil Stein as league commissioner in 1992. The reverse logos of the game pucks were updated to feature his signature. Mr. Bettman appears tp be finicky about his signature and updated it twice. The middle version shown recently surfaced.

During this time period, In Glas Co produced several slug variations finally settling on a version with an NHL logo on the side of the puck.

At the bottom of this page is an unusual puck issued on a Czechoslovakian slug with this game puck back. The 75th Anniversary has been found issued with the orange back.
