NORTHEASTERN JUNIOR HOCKEY LEAGUE HISTORY

1983 - 1997

 

At this time, little is known about the Northeastern Junior Hockey League. It changed its name to the Empire Junior 'B' Hockey League in 1997. The Wheatfield Blades joined the league back in 1987. A program has been found for the Green Machine of Long Island and they competed against the Buffalo Flyers along with teams from Lockport, Rochester, Cheektowaga and Amherst.
