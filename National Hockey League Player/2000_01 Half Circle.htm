IN GLAS CO - SEMI-CIRCLE SERIES

(2000 - 2001)

In Glas Co issued a series of player pucks with a color picture of the player in half circle on the left side of the puck. Most pucks had the reverse logo shown. A few special pucks commemorating player milestones featured unique backs (They are noted with the milestone below the players name on the plaque. To see the reverse, hold the cursor over the image.).
