Omnitrade Reverse

(1992 - 1993)

A new league logo was issued in 1992. The first series of pucks with this new logo was issued by Omnitrade. It's also the first series with the notation 'Official Licensed Product' on the back. Czechoslovakian slugs were used. Two of the pucks shown (Atlanta and the 1993 All-Star Game) had back variations and to see their backs, hold the cursor over the image until it appears.
