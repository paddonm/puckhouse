Nestor Johnson - IHL Crossed Sticks Reverse

(1962 - 1963)

The Nestor Johnson Company of Chicago, Illinois issued pucks for the IHL in the early 1960's. This series featured rubberized logos with a black background and red logos from and back. Many of the teams that had Nestor pucks in the IHL ended up in the CHL in 1963. Not surprising, the Nestor slug was the first puck of the CHL as a result.
