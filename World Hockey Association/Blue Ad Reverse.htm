Colored Biltrite Advertisement Reverse

(1972 - 1976)

 

The first game pucks used by the WHA were the blue Biltrite slugs with the WHA logo on the back. The blue slug was supposed to make the puck easier to see on the ice and on TV. This made no difference and the league switched to traditional black pucks after a couple of months. The blue pucks, with advertisements on the backs are very hard to come by. To see the ad on the back, hold the cursor over the desired puck.
